BEST SNACK: DRIED PERSIMMON!

WHAT

Dried persimmon is a traditional dried fruit snack produced in East Asia. Covered by a light and white coating of sugar, the inner part of the dried persimmon gives the eater a soft, chewy, and sweet sense.

Close Look at Delicious Persimmon

WHERE

Many Asian countries (such as China, Japan, Korea etc) produce and sell dried persimmon in winter. According to different countries' geography, the shape and the taste of the persimmon varies.

A Picture of Showing The Production of Dried Persimmon

WHY

There are many benefits of consuming dried persimmon:

1. Enhancement of immunity due to its great embodiment of Vitamin C.

2. Provision of quick energy through its supply of fructose, which is the most easily assimilatable food for cells.

3. Improvement of eyesight and cell growth due to its supply of Vitamin A.

4. Supply of a great amount of happiness!
